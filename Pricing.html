Frequently asked

The questions we hear most

If you have one that isn’t here, it’s the kind of thing the demo is built to answer

Do you offer a free trial?

No - and here’s the honest reason. Digital Hive connects to your actual BI environment. A sandbox trial tells you nothing meaningful about what the platform will do for your specific setup. Our solution demo is more useful: it runs live against multiple BI, AI, and content platforms so you can see exactly what a deployment in your environment looks like

Do admins or executives cost more than standard users?

No. One flat rate per active user. No role tiers, no administrator markup, no internal-versus-external splits. A user is a user. Pricing is predictable

Does my deployment option affect pricing?

Deployment type - on-premises, private cloud or public cloud - does not change the per-user rate

What does implementation involve?

Most implementations are live within weeks, not months. Your existing BI tools stay in place - the implementation is platform installation, connector configuration and hive design, not tool migration. Implementation services are scoped per project and can be delivered by Digital Hive or a certified partner

Are there setup or onboarding fees?

There are no surprise add-ons in a Digital Hive proposal. Implementation services, when scoped, are clearly itemized

What’s the minimum commitment?

Proposals are scoped based on your proposed deployment - active user count, and access requirements. We can discuss your situation and follow with a proposal
