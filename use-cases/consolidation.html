What actually happens

The consolidation conversation that never closes

The tool count grew over years - one per team, one from an acquisition, one a business unit stood up on its own. Every budget cycle, leadership asks about consolidation.

The conversation ends the same way: nobody knows what's being used. Licenses renew. Costs compound.

Why this doesn't get fixed

Why the decision never gets made

  • Consolidation requires certainty no one has: which reports are used, how often, by whom.
  • That data lives in five separate admin consoles, in five different formats.
  • Every platform has a champion. Without usage data, those arguments can't be countered.
  • The risk of cutting too fast is real. Without evidence, no one will take it.
What changes

How Digital Hive solves it

Four shifts that turn consolidation from a debate into a decision.

Cross-platform usage in one view

Which reports are opened, how often, by whom - across every connected platform.

Abandoned content surfaces

Duplicates and unused reports identified and flagged. Platform champions see the same numbers everyone else sees.

Evidence replaces politics

Consolidation sequenced on usage data. Content with zero views loses its shield.

Phased, invisible execution

The access layer remains as platforms are retired. Users find everything in the same place.
