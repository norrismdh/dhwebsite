What actually happens

The daily search that shouldn't exist.

Users open a browser to find a report. Then they open another tab, and another. Then they try Slack or Teams. Then email a colleague, then submit a ticket, then they build it themselves in Excel.

This happens dozens of times a day across a typical enterprise - and the compounded productivity loss is significant.

Why this doesn't get fixed

Why better folders don't solve it.

  • Every BI platform has its own search. Tableau doesn't find Power BI reports. Power BI doesn't find Looker content.
  • Users must know which tool to search before they can search it.
  • Central catalog initiatives fail because maintenance never gets prioritized.
  • Discovery depends on personal networks. New employees and siloed teams lose.
What changes

How Digital Hive solves it.

Four shifts that make analytics findable for everyone.

One search bar. Every tool. Every report.

Digital Hive indexes all connected BI platforms and surfaces everything in one place.

Certified content first.

Reviewed, owned, validated content is visually distinct and ranked above unverified content.

SSO across platforms.

No re-authentication per tool. Access governed by permissions already defined in each source system.

A catalog that improves over time.

Usage signals surface popular content and flag stale reports automatically.
