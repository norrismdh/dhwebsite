What actually happens

A catalog that grew without anyone managing it

Every team had a reason to build something. Projects created reports that outlived the projects. Initiatives produced dashboards accessed by no one. Turnover left content with no owners

The catalog is technically searchable but practically unusable - finding the right report requires already knowing what to look for

Why this doesn't get fixed

Why the catalog never gets cleaned up

  • Usage data stays siloed within each BI platform. No cross-platform view exists
  • Catalog maintenance is assigned to someone as an afterthought, or to no one
  • Deprecation is political without evidence. Report owners resist decommissioning what they built
  • Trust signals are invisible. Every report looks equally unreliable
