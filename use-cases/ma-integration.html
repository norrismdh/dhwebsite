What actually happens

The first week after close

Two BI environments land in the same organization overnight. Both have a report called "Revenue Summary." Neither number matches. IT is in triage. Executives can't get answers. Your users can't see their content.

And IT doesn't have six months to rebuild everything before the business needs to move.

Why this doesn't get fixed

Three reasons this stays unresolved

  • Integration gets scoped as a migration - which takes months and leaves both sides in limbo.
  • No neutral ground: one company's tools win, the other loses access until IT catches up.
  • Governance can't wait, but integrating two security models across two platforms is a project on its own.
What changes

How Digital Hive solves it

Four shifts. No migration required.

Unified access layer - day one

Digital Hive indexes content from both environments. One search. Everything visible.

Existing credentials, existing permissions

Users on both sides use their current credentials. No new provisioning. No new security model.

Side-by-side reconciliation

Metric conflicts surface immediately - not in a board meeting three months later.

Governance centralized, not rebuilt

Both security models inherited and enforced through one layer from day one.
