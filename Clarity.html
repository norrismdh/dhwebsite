The outcome

Thriving, not just surviving.

When COVID battered the travel sector, Clarity leaned into Digital Hive instead of pulling back. It moved customers and staff to self-service analytics, automated its finance reporting, and shipped innovative ESG travel metrics-turning a survival project into a growth engine that now touches every team in the company.

Based in Manchester, Clarity is a multi-award-winning corporate travel management company whose mission is to help businesses track, control and manage travel spend. Its ClarityInsights family of solutions-part of it built on Digital Hive-helps clients manage everything from air, rail, hotel and events to visas and traveller support, all in one place.

The challenge

The greatest threat in a century of trading.

A company built to weather good times and bad met a downturn unlike any before it-and had to keep clients close while costs came down hard.

A sector in free fall

COVID grounded business travel almost overnight. For a travel-management firm, it was an existential test of how fast the business could adapt.

Costs down, analysts furloughed

Streamlining meant putting many MI analysts onto furlough-just as customers needed answers faster than ever before.

Clients needed self-service, now

Customers had to see refund and cancellation status instantly and cut their own costs-without waiting on a manual report.

The solution

ClarityInsights: one portal over every analytics tool.

Digital Hive sits above Clarity's existing BI stack-unifying Cognos, Microsoft Reporting Services, Qlik Sense and ThoughtSpot into one personalized, governed portal. Nothing migrated. Nothing rebuilt.

01 / UNIFIED PORTAL

Personalized views of every data asset.

Business users and customers get tailored access to the reports and dashboards that matter to them-across multiple ABI tools, in one place.

02 / SEARCH-DRIVEN ANALYTICS

Ask in plain language. Get instant answers.

Digital Hive serves ThoughtSpot's search-driven analytics on tap-so users explore live, interactive pinboards and real-time data using natural language.

03 / CUSTOM GAMEBOARDS

A purpose-built view for every team.

Highly customized gameboards give specialist teams-Finance, Marketing, Commercial-exactly the analytics they need and nothing they don't.

04 / LOW-CODE BUILD

Design and build at the same time.

Fast low-code development lets Clarity model, create and change a personalized client portal quickly-cutting cost and effort dramatically.

Before / After

The business, before and after the pivot.

Same data. Same team. A very different operating model.

Before the acceleration
  • MI analysts produced reports by hand for clients and internal teams.
  • Customers waited on manual reports to see refund and cancellation status.
  • Analytics lived in separate tools-Cognos, Qlik, MS Reporting-siloed.
  • No natural-language way to explore data without an analyst's help.
  • Carbon and ESG reporting was limited and hard to surface.
  • The "end-to-end" vision for analytics remained just that-a vision.
With Digital Hive
  • Customers and staff self-serve analytics on tap-no analyst bottleneck.
  • Instant refund and cancellation views, built the moment they were needed.
  • One portal over every ABI tool, with personalized views per team.
  • Natural-language search via ThoughtSpot-ask a question, get an answer.
  • On-screen carbon-offset metrics help clients meet ESG commitments.
  • ClarityInsights now runs end-to-end-touching every area of the business.
