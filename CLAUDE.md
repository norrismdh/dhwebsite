# Digital Hive Website — Claude Guidelines

## Copy & Punctuation

- **No trailing periods on UI copy.** Headlines, subheadings, short paragraphs, bullet points, card text, CTA labels, and any other marketing/UI copy must not end with a period. This applies to all marketing pages.
- Exception: blog articles, legal pages (Privacy.html, Terms.html, nda*.html), and long-form resources are prose — keep standard punctuation there.
