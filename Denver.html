The outcome

The ultimate research assistant.

The University of Denver had a set of analytics and reporting tools that weren't playing nicely together. Digital Hive's design and mobile responsiveness gave admin and academic functions an easy-to-use, system-agnostic, one-stop-shop for their information—and DU began replacing its university-wide performance dashboard with it.

Long a champion of data-driven decisions through its Information Measurement and Analytics Council, DU wanted a clean interface to trusted information that the whole institution could rely on—without forcing anyone off the reporting tools they already used. One demo was all it took for the team to see Digital Hive was the one.

The challenge

Good tools. Just not playing nicely.

DU had no shortage of analytics and reporting tools—what it lacked was one trustworthy place to find and use them.

Tools that didn't talk

A patchwork of analytics and reporting systems across the university that weren't playing nicely—each an island of its own.

No single front door

Admin and academic functions alike had nowhere easy, system-agnostic, and mobile-friendly to go for the information they needed.

Decisions need trusted data

The Analytics Council championed data-driven decisions—but only if the underlying data was easy to find, trust, and act on.

The solution

One system-agnostic home for every tool.

Digital Hive sits above DU's existing reporting tools, data systems and knowledge base—giving the whole university one clean, mobile-ready interface to trusted information. Nothing migrated. Nothing rebuilt.

01 / ONE-STOP-SHOP

An easy-to-use insight hub.

A single, system-agnostic home for admin and academic functions—so anyone can find the dashboards and reports they need without hunting.

02 / MOBILE RESPONSIVE

Trusted information, anywhere.

A clean, responsive design that works across devices—so the research assistant is just as usable on a phone between classes as at a desk.

03 / FEDERATED SEARCH

Search every system at once.

Search for data assets across all information systems—with links straight to the supporting documentation in the knowledge base.

04 / FUTURE-PROOF

Add a tool. Everything still works.

Upgrade any reporting tool—or add Power BI or Tableau later—and it all keeps working together. No rip-and-replace, ever.
