About

We built the thing analytics teams were asking for and nobody was building.

Digital Hive started from a simple observation: enterprises were adding BI tools faster than users could keep up. The catalog was the missing layer.

Origin

The category nobody else had reason to build.

Digital Hive emerged from a recognition that the analytics catalog - the layer that sits above BI tools and makes them usable at scale - was a critical gap. BI vendors had no incentive to fill it; their interests stopped at the edges of their own platform.

So we built it. A vendor-neutral layer that indexes, organizes, and surfaces analytics from every tool an enterprise already owns - without touching the source. Today, Digital Hive operates as the category-defining analytics catalog for multi-vendor, multi-tenant, and complex enterprise analytics environments.

DH Vendor-neutral by design - we never compete with the BI tools we connect to.

How we operate

Independent. Bootstrapped. Built for the long run.

Digital Hive is an independent software company building governance and operations tooling for enterprise analytics environments. No platform allegiance. No upstream pressure to favor one BI vendor over another. Our incentive is the same as yours: make the tools you already own work better together.
