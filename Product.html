Analytics Hub & Discovery

Find and understand analytics across every system

Digital Hive acts as a unified analytics hub — indexing content across BI tools, documents, and enterprise systems into a single, searchable experience. It captures metadata — ownership, certification status, usage data — and makes every asset searchable in one place.

  • Global search across BI platforms and content systems
  • Metadata-driven discovery (definitions, ownership, usage)
  • Personalized access (favorites, recommendations)
  • Cross-platform usage visibility (popularity, engagement, relevance)
  • Support for structured and unstructured content (AI agents, BI, documents, video, external feeds)

For the record

Let's be direct.

What Digital Hive is — and just as importantly, what it isn't.

Not a BI tool

We don't build reports

We don't replace Power BI, Tableau, or anything else. Those tools stay.

Not a data catalog

Not built for data engineers

Data catalogs serve people building pipelines. Digital Hive serves the people who consume analytics.

Not a migration tool

We don't migrate dashboards

Migration happens in the background. Users never feel the disruption — the portal stays consistent while IT works underneath.
