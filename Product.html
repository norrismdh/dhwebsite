The catalog

One inventory. Every tool. Zero rebuilding.

Digital Hive indexes reports and dashboards from every connected BI platform. It captures metadata - ownership, certification status, usage data, lineage - and makes every asset searchable in one place.

  • Automated metadata harvesting No manual tagging required to get started - connectors pull metadata directly from each source platform.
  • Certification and ownership visibility Users know what to trust instantly. Every asset shows who owns it and whether it's certified.
  • Usage telemetry See what's actually being used, and what isn't. Stale assets surface themselves - retirement becomes obvious.

The BI Hub

Analytics organized around how your business works - not which tool built it.

Most organizations organize analytics by platform. Business users think in functions, teams, and outcomes. The BI Hub delivers analytics through a branded, role-personalized experience. Finance sees finance. Sales sees sales. Executives see what drives strategy.

  • Customizable Hives per team, role, or business unit Each audience sees a curated home. Same source. Different lens.
  • No-code layout builder for hub admins Drag, drop, publish. Admins reshape Hives without touching a line of code.
  • Single sign-on - one login, every tool Users authenticate once. From there, every connected platform opens without re-login.
  • Branded experience - your logo, your colors The Hub looks like your company, not ours. White-label down to the favicon.

Security

Enterprise security. No new attack surface.

Digital Hive is a metadata layer. Reports and data stay where they are. Permissions stay where they are. Authentication uses what you already trust.

View Trust Center Download Security Datasheet
  • Source security inherited Permissions defined in each BI tool are respected. Digital Hive never overwrites them.
  • No data movement Reports and data stay in native platforms. Digital Hive indexes metadata only.
  • SSO and MFA Enterprise authentication via SAML, OIDC, and Active Directory. Sessions, scopes, and audit logs included.
For the record

Worth saying plainly.

What Digital Hive is - and just as importantly, what it isn't.

Not a BI tool

We don't build reports.

We don't replace Power BI, Tableau, or anything else. Those tools stay.

Not a data catalog

Not built for data engineers.

Data catalogs serve people building pipelines. Digital Hive serves the people who consume analytics.

Not a migration tool

We don't migrate dashboards.

We make migration invisible to users while IT works behind the scenes. Platforms swap underneath; the Hive stays.
