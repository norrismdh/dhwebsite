Where Digital Hive leads

Three areas where buyers consistently pick us

  • Enterprise procurement readiness

    SOC 2 Type II, ISO 27001, and HIPAA-ready out of the box. The security questionnaire is short because the architecture is simple: metadata-only indexing, native auth passthrough, no data movement. Less proof-of-life work for your procurement and architecture teams

  • Migration and version coexistence

    Built around situations where the analytics stack is in motion - platform migrations, post-acquisition integrations, parallel old-and-new environments. Documentation, customer references, and use-case depth concentrate here

  • Catalog-first discovery

    Search across every connected tool is the first action in the product, not a configuration. Certified content surfaces above uncertified by default - users are guided to what to trust without rules they have to read

Where Metric Insights may fit better

Situations where their approach is the closer match

  • Active push distribution

    If your top requirement is reaching users where they already work - email digests, SMS alerts, mobile push for KPI thresholds - Metric Insights' alerting and distribution layer is ahead. Digital Hive is a pull experience; Metric Insights pushes

  • Mobile-first or field-based users

    Native iOS and Android apps matter when your audience is on the road, on a hospital floor, or on a trading desk that needs alerts off-laptop. Responsive web isn't always enough; if it's not, Metric Insights' native apps are the closer fit
