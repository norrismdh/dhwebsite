    Mutual Non-Disclosure & Confidentiality Agreement

    This is a summary of the agreement you will be asked to sign. The full, legally binding text — including the signature blocks — is presented on the secure signing page after you submit your details.

    Parties

    This Agreement is between Digital Hive Software, Inc., a Texas corporation located at 6600 Chase Oaks Boulevard, Suite 150, Plano, TX 75023, and its subsidiaries and affiliates (“Digital Hive”), and your company (“Company”), together the “Parties.”

    1. Definitions

    “Proprietary Information” covers both Confidential Information (non-public business information that is marked confidential or would reasonably be understood to be confidential) and Trade Secrets as defined under the Texas Uniform Trade Secrets Act. Digital Hive’s Trade Secrets include all components of its software products, documentation, help content, and any ideas, algorithms, or information embodied in them.

    2. Rights & Obligations

    Each Party receiving Proprietary Information agrees to:

    1. Treat all Proprietary Information as strictly confidential and use it only for the purpose of the potential business relationship.
    2. Not use the information to compete with the disclosing Party.
    3. Only share it with representatives who have a need to know and are bound by these same obligations.
    4. Not use the information to train or improve any AI, machine-learning, or large language model system, and not upload it to any third-party AI system unless that provider is bound by equally restrictive terms.
    5. Promptly cease use and return or destroy all copies upon request, and certify destruction.
    6. Exercise commercially reasonable care to protect the information.

    Standard exceptions apply for information that becomes public through no fault of the receiving Party, was already lawfully known, or is received from a third party without a duty of confidentiality.

    3. Remedies

    The Parties agree that a breach would cause irreparable harm. The disclosing Party is entitled to injunctive relief (without bond) in addition to damages, and the prevailing Party in any dispute is entitled to reasonable attorney’s fees and costs.

    4. Term & Termination

    Confidential Information is protected for at least five (5) years from disclosure; Trade Secrets are protected in perpetuity or until they no longer qualify under law. The Agreement runs for one (1) year, renews automatically for successive one-year terms, and either Party may terminate on sixty (60) days’ written notice.

    5–11. Additional Terms

    The Agreement also covers export compliance, assignability, severability, the validity of electronic signatures, entire-agreement and amendment terms, Texas choice of law and venue (Dallas County), and obligations regarding subpoenas or legal demands for the information.

    The summary above is provided for convenience only and does not modify the binding terms of the Agreement presented for signature.
